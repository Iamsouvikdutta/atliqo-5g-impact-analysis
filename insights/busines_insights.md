# Business Insights & Recommendations

## Revenue Insights

- Overall revenue remained relatively stable after the 5G launch, declining marginally by 0.50%.
- Revenue growth was concentrated in a limited number of high-performing cities.
- Premium plan adoption positively influenced ARPU growth.

## Customer Insights

- Active users declined by 8.28%, indicating customer retention challenges.
- Several cities experienced monetization-driven growth, where higher ARPU offset declining customer numbers.
- Certain markets demonstrated acquisition opportunities with growing user bases.

## Market Share Insights

- Market share movements varied significantly across cities.
- Competitive pressure remains high in strategically important markets.
- Early identification of declining market share is critical to prevent long-term erosion.

## Churn Insights

- High-value markets with increasing churn require immediate intervention.
- Customer retention initiatives should be prioritized over aggressive acquisition in these regions.
- Sustained churn growth may negatively impact future revenue stability.

## Strategic Recommendations

### 1. Strengthen Retention Programs

Focus retention efforts in high-revenue cities exhibiting increasing churn to protect existing revenue streams.

### 2. Expand Premium Plan Adoption

Leverage successful premium plans to further improve ARPU while ensuring customer value perception remains strong.

### 3. Target Acquisition Opportunity Markets

Invest in cities showing positive user growth but lower monetization potential.

### 4. Monitor Competitor Activity

Develop proactive market monitoring mechanisms to protect market share in strategically important regions.

### 5. Prioritize Data-Driven Decision Making

Use churn trends, revenue growth, and market segmentation frameworks to guide future 5G expansion strategies.
